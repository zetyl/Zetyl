Common Mistakes Business Websites Make

Most websites do not fail because they exist. They fail because they confuse people.

A business website should make people understand what you do, trust you faster, and take action without friction.

01Outdated Design

Visitors judge credibility quickly when a site looks old.

02Poor Mobile Experience

Most customers check businesses from their phones first.

03Weak Calls To Action

If people do not know what to do next, they leave.

04Slow Loading Pages

Speed affects trust, patience, and conversion.

05Unclear Service Information

Customers should understand your services without searching.

06No Trust Structure

A good site uses layout, wording, and visuals to build confidence.
